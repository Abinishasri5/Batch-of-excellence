Our Mission

This analytics portal was developed to provide comprehensive insights into our research data through interactive visualizations and dashboards. Our team has worked diligently to create a platform that makes complex data accessible and actionable for decision-makers.

The project leverages IBM Cognos Analytics to deliver powerful business intelligence capabilities, combined with a custom web interface for enhanced usability and accessibility.

Our goal is to empower users with data-driven insights that can drive innovation, improve efficiency, and reveal new opportunities for growth and development.

Technology Stack

Frontend: HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, Font Awesome

Analytics: IBM Cognos Analytics

Hosting: Web server with HTTPS support

Security: Role-based access control, encrypted communications
